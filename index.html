Ember on Occasion

The year is 2024. You are 8 hours into Cyberpunk 2077, having waited until it went on sale. Life took you away for most of last year, but you've got 30 minutes on a Sunday night to hop back in. But you don't remember any of the buttons, and there's no way you're starting over to replay the tutorial. You give up and wash some dishes.

If you don't work with Ember.js every day but enjoy leveraging it for the occasional project, you don't need the story-mode lessons of the Ember.js guides. This site is a work-in-progress designed to be a collection of quick references and patterns useful for the casual, infrequent developer using Ember.js.

The focus will be on quick "how did I do that" usage of Ember.js, including the references required for import in case your editor of choice doesn't handle language servers perfectly.

These docs are generally up-to-date for Ember.js 7.1.
